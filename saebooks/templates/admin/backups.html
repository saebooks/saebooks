How this works

A systemd user timer runs scripts/backup.sh every night at 02:15 and writes a gzipped pg_dump to {{ summary.backup_dir }}. Retention: 7 daily + 4 weekly (Sunday) + 12 monthly (1st-of-month).

A second timer runs scripts/restore-test.sh every Sunday at 03:15 — it restores the latest dump into a scratch database and asserts row counts against the live DB (±5% drift tolerance). Failures fire a Telegram notification via claude-notify.

Manual runs from the host: ~/projects/saebooks/scripts/backup.sh or ...restore-test.sh.
