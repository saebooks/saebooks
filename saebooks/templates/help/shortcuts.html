Keyboard shortcuts

KeyWhat it does
Ctrl + K or /Open the global search palette
g dGo to Dashboard
g jGo to Journal
g iGo to Invoices
g bGo to Bills
g cGo to Contacts
g aGo to Accounts
g rGo to Reports
g pGo to Payments
nNew record on the current list page (where supported)
EscClose palette / dialog

Shortcuts only fire when no input, textarea, or select is focused — so typing inside a form never triggers navigation.
