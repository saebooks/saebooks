A partial disposal splits this asset in two. The parent keeps the retained share; a new child row is created carrying the disposed fraction's cost and gets disposed in a single journal (DR cash + DR accum-dep, CR cost, with gain/loss plugging 4-9100 or 6-9100). Depreciation is first caught up to the disposal date so the shares are computed against a current NBV. Use this when you've sold part of a multi-unit asset (e.g. 3 of 10 laptops purchased on one invoice).
