all marketing copy must hold): SAE Books is software. It computes from the
customer's data; the customer (or their registered agent) prepares and
lodges their own obligations and makes their own declarations; SAE Books
transmits as the customer's digital service provider and never acts as tax
agent, BAS agent, or adviser. Features must be designed so this stays true
— anything that would put SAE Books in the preparer's seat is a business
decision requiring Tax Agent Services Act analysis first, not a feature
request.
